Frequently Asked Questions?

PureMatch Halal isn’t a dating app/site. We are a faith-based matchmaking for individuals seeking meaningful connections. We offer a personalised service, where we get to know each and every client, in order to find a suitable match. You'll speak to us 1-2-1 and we will get to understand who you are and what you're looking for, and match you accordingly.

All informations are kept confidential and no information is shared publicly. PureMatch Halal ensures that we are the only people to have access to your private information, and create Summary Profiles for other candidates to see, which contains no sensitive information. We do take in to account a candidates preferences, so if there is anything they wished to have excluded or included in their SP, we are happy to go along with their wishes. 

A Mini Profile is what is shown to prospective matches. It's used to give each candidate a glimpse in to a potential match and used as a tool to decide whether or not to take the match forward or not.
