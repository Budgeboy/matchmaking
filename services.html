FILL UP A REGISTRATON FORM
Register With Us!

Complete Registration Form Online. This will tell us what you want. Click here to register. A mini profile MP will be produced. It will contain: - Your basic details - Photo (optional) - About Me - What I am Looking for

INITIAL CONTACT
Wait For Us to Get In Touch With You!

We will arrange the 1-2-1 Consultation meeting. Via:-Facetime /WhatsApp Video Call. The 1-2-1 Consultation is not an interview, but an informal call so that we can get to know one another better.

LET'S FIND YOU A MATCH
Let's Search For You!

We will only introduce 1 Potential Match at a time to avoid conflict of interest. This will give you both an equal chance of really getting to know one another!. We will initially send both members a MP before hand to see if you both consent. If yes, numbers will be exchanged at that point and the rest is up to you!

Personal 1-to-1 CONSULTATION
Personal Consultation With Us!

At the 1-to-1 Consultation, we are really interested in getting to know you and the kind of potential you are essentially looking for!. We will also send you your MP via email (please allow 48 hours) after the consultation has taken place, so that you can approve the document. Please Note: We will NEVER ask to take a photograph of your ID - but we will need to see it in order to verify your identity for the safety of all our members.

KEEPING IN TOUCH
Keep In Touch With Us!

If there are any issues or concerns, we are always on hand to offer our support and guidance. If it so happens that you are both incompatible - feedback will be taken from both members and noted. A new match will then be introduced via the same process.

MATCHMADE IN HEAVEN
You Got A Match!

In Sha Allah, our aim is to find all our members suitable spouses. We genuinely want to help our members. Therefore we need to form great partnerships to help one another. At this point we wish you the best of luck with your future and our duas are with you, we pray it is a successful marriage. Ameen.
