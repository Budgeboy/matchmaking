Annual Fee

If you wish to stay with us, we charge an annual renewal fee of £50 for those in the diaspora and #50,000 for those in Nigeria from the date you registered. We will contact you when your registration needs renewing. Members who wish to leave the service with no successful match after the year has elapsed are free to leave - all information and photos of the candidate will be destroyed safely and securely. An exit interview may be conducted. 

Processing Payments

Once we receive and process your Registration Form, we will then invoice (request) payment from you via Paypal. or bank transfer Details of payment will be sent to you via the email address you provided us with on the Registration Form.

Please use PMH as reference to the payment being made to us. Please kindly make payment as soon as possible - so that it does not hold up the registration process. Once payment is received we can then arrange the 1-2-1 Consultation. Thank you.

Rules

  1. 1-2-1 Consultation Appointments will then be set up weekdays from 6pm to 11pm , therefore we need the lines of communication open with our members so that we keep you informed every step of the way during the process.
  2. We will only introduce 1 Potential Match at a time, so that you can both really get to know one other, to avoid any confusion. We will keep in touch on a weekly basis to see how things are progressing.
  3. We will offer our support and guidance and therefore give you the opportunity to keep in touch with us if you please - we are just a phone call or email away.
  4. If your personal details or circumstances happen to change during the process, we expect you to inform us so that we can update your details accordingly.
  5. We will not tolerate any abusive behaviour from any of our members and expect everyone to treat one another with respect and dignity.
  6. We are looking for members who are genuinely interested in finding a companion and are willing to work with us. We initially require you to fill out a Registration Form, attached with a photograph.
